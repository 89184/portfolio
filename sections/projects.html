AgriBoot – AI-Powered Agricultural Advisory System

Problem Solved

Farmers face challenges in accessing reliable and timely agricultural information. Agricultural knowledge is distributed across numerous research papers, government guidelines, and expert resources.

Solution

Developed an AI-powered agricultural advisory platform using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), LangChain, FAISS, and LLMs. Built a semantic search engine capable of understanding natural language agricultural queries.

Impact
  • Indexed 1,400+ agricultural documents into 5,200+ searchable knowledge chunks
  • Reduced information retrieval time by 80%+, from minutes to seconds
  • Delivered instant recommendations on crop management, fertilizers, diseases, and government schemes
  • Resulted in a Research Publication and Software Copyright Registration
Tech Stack: Python, LangChain, FAISS, Hugging Face, SQLite, CustomTkinter
